Comparing A2C and PPO with different network polices using snake game

Deep reinforcement learning algorithms like Advantage Actor-critic (A2C) and Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) have recently become popular and widespread for game AI. Identifying the best algorithm and neural network policy for the custom environment is challenging. A2C can have problems where an actor's chosen action is heavily influenced by their training trajectory, making them poor at exploration. Main reason for selecting A2C and PPO as the main algorithms to compare in this project is due to its similarities. A2C and PPO are compared using three different training models to determine the optimum algorithm and policy.
